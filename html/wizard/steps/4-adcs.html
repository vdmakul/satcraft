Attitude Determination and Control subsystem

If your payload or communicartions system requires your sattelite to be in a specific orientation, e.g. constantly facing the earth with a camera. Then you need a ADCS subsystem. The ADCS handles the two main functions need for orienting your satellite: measurement of the orientation and manuvering capabilites.
